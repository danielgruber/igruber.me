Daniel Gruber

Daniel is the founder of the CMS Goma, which won the first price in the competition "Jugend Forscht" 2013, and he loves to work as Frontend-Webdesigner and Developer.

At the moment he is working on a new project called PaperOrganizer, which was launched in March 2014 and is a consumer-solution to intelligently organize your documents.

He lives in Germering and has completed secondary school with the german "Abitur" at Max-Born-Gymnasium in May 2014.

Since Fall 2014, Daniel has been studying business informatics at TU Munich.
